Welcome to the 2003 Editor Project

This is the central hub for the upcoming custom build of the leaked Hammer Editor, specifically stabilized and tailored for the legendary 2003 leaked build of Half-Life 2 (WC Mappack / Anon Leak).

Current Status

Our goal is to create a functioning mapping environment that preserves the historical accuracy of the 2003 Source Engine tools, whilst fixing the most notorious crash-to-desktop (CTD) bugs that plagued the original leak.

Planned Features:

  • Fixed entity properties crashes.
  • Restored missing 2003 FGD definitions.
  • Stability improvements for massive WC Mappack VMFs.
  • Properly compiling BSPs directly to the leaked build formats.
